# Tenebris Prime

**[ALPHA - Major Revamp]** Explore Tenebris, a dark rogue planet enshrouded in a thick, corrosive atmosphere. Harness piezoelectric crystals and thermal systems to survive in this unforgiving world.

## Overview

Tenebris is a world of eternal darkness where conventional power generation fails. Solar panels are useless, and even night vision cannot pierce the gloom. Survival requires mastering new technologies unique to this hostile environment.

## Key Features

### Piezoelectric Power
- Capture wild **quartz ortets** from crystal forests using capture bot rockets
- Converted ortets become **piezoelectric generators** that provide power
- Generator output scales with the maturity of the captured crystal
- No electric poles allowed - they explode on placement!

### Thermal Systems
- **Thermal Diodes** - Control heat flow direction for efficient thermal networks
- **Thermal Batteries** - Store and release heat energy
- **Heated Atmosphere Scrubbers** - Clear toxic spores from the air

### Corrosive Atmosphere
- **Cargo Pod Corrosion** - Basic materials corrode during descent
  - Batteries and scrap cause explosions
  - Iron/copper components convert to waste
  - Finished machines and modules are safe
- **Banned Entities** - Many basic entities explode on placement
  - All electric poles
  - Basic pipes (use biopipes instead)
  - Lower-tier belts and inserters
  - Assembling machine 1, iron chest, steam engine

### Spore Clearance
- Toxic spores damage exposed machinery
- Build heated atmosphere scrubbers to clear spores
- Non-heated machines require sufficient spore clearance to operate

### Tenespace
- Navigate through dangerous space locations to reach Tenebris
- Centipedes spawn on platforms traversing tenespace
- Ambient particle effects and corrosion on unprotected platforms

### Deep Space Sensing
- Launch observation satellites to discover hidden deep space objects
- Distance affects discovery time - choose launch locations strategically
